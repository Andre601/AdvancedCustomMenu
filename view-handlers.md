# View Handlers #
***
```yaml
View-Handlers:
  Permission: 'example.savage'
  Balance: 50
```
### Summary ###
- [Permission](#permission)
- [Balance](#balance)

***
- #### Permission ####
***
  Info: The Permission the player must have to view an item    
  Default Value: none  
  
***
- #### Balance ####
***
  Info: The amount of *money* a player must have in their account to view an item    
  Default Value: none
